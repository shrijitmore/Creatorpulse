# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome contributors of all backgrounds and experience levels
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not people
- Accept that maintainers have final say on project direction

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Trolling or sustained disruption of discussions
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to orangeglazz@gmail.com. Maintainers will review and respond within 72 hours. Consequences range from a warning to a permanent ban, depending on severity.
